BUY COFFEE & TEA ONLINE

At J. Hornig you can order your favourite coffee online around the clock. Whether whole coffee beans, ground coffee, cold brew or coffee capsules. You’ll also find tea at J. Hornig: Double-chamber and organic triangle shaped teas in many different varieties can be found in our online shop. In Austria and Germany we also ship your coffee order free of shipping costs for orders over € 20.

J. HORNIG SPECIALITY COFFEE

J. Hornig offers you a range of Single Origin Direct-Trade Specialty Coffees to choose from. This coffee comes directly from the farmer to us in Graz for roasting without any detours. This guarantees the highest coffee quality and better conditions for our farmers.

TRADITIONAL LONG-TERM ROASTING

When roasting our coffee beans, we swear by traditional long-term roasting. It is only through this gentle roasting process that the taste potential of the roasted coffee is fully exploited. This comparatively long roasting time ensures that chlorogenic acid and bitter substances are broken down. This makes our coffee particularly digestible.

MAKES MORE OF TIME

At J. Hornig, we believe that things really get good when you do them carefully and with great attention to detail. We roast our coffee – with one decisive difference to our competitors: we take our time. Using traditional long-term roasting, each of our beans gets exactly the time it needs to develop the perfect taste. And that’s what you taste.
